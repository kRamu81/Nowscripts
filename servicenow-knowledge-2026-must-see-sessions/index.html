ServiceNow Knowledge 2026 Must-See Sessions

By Christine Horton

April 28, 2026

ServiceNow Knowledge 2026 is almost here, bringing together customers, partners, developers, architects, and platform leaders for one of the biggest weeks in the ServiceNow calendar.

We asked the community which sessions they’re most excited about – from AI strategy and product innovation to hands-on technical learning and peer insights. And if you’re not attending in person, you can still follow key announcements and selected sessions through the event livestream and digital coverage.

Main Keynote

The headline session this year is Welcome to Agentic Business, where CEO Bill McDermott and ServiceNow leaders are expected to outline the company’s next phase of AI-native platform strategy. 

With AI, data, governance, workflows, and security becoming more deeply integrated, this keynote should set the tone for what customers and partners can expect across the platform in 2026 and beyond.

READ MORE: ServiceNow Knowledge 2026 Lineup: Idris Elba, Mindy Kaling, Jensen Huang, and More

Don’t Miss CreatorCon

Alongside these Knowledge sessions, don’t forget CreatorCon – the must-see part of Knowledge for builders, architects, admins, and developers who want practical value beyond the keynote stage. 

It’s where ServiceNow pros get hands-on with platform innovation, technical deep dives, labs, and real-world build sessions you can take straight back to your organization.

“I am looking forward to joining the MVP CreatorCon Summit before Knowledge on May 4, participating in the hackathon, spending time at the booth, exploring the event, and attending some great sessions on AI Agents, Control Tower, and the Vibe Code Lounge.”

Mahathi Veena, ServiceNow CTA & MVP

Build Your Agenda Early

Use the Knowledge Agenda Builder early and build with intent – start by locking in your must-attend keynotes, then reserve high-demand CreatorCon labs, hands-on sessions, and technical deep dives before they fill up. 

Balance your schedule with a mix of strategy sessions, practical workshops, and networking time, and leave a little white space for expo discoveries, last-minute recommendations you’ll hear onsite – and of course the parties!

Day 1: Tuesday, May 5

Building an AI-Ready Workforce: A Cross-Industry Roundtable

AI is rapidly changing the way we work, and it is presenting leaders with a question: how can we prepare our workforce for the AI-driven future? This roundtable brings together experts from different industries who are confronting the same question.

Add to agenda

Context Engine and Context Layer: What Is It and Why Does It Matter?

Are you a bit unclear about what people mean when you hear terms like “semantic layers” or “context engine”? If you have questions, this session should help provide you with answers, while laying out ServiceNow’s cognitive intelligence vision.

Add to agenda

Now on Now: From Blind Spots to Control – Securing Non-Human Identities With Veza

Veza was one of ServiceNow’s biggest acquisitions of all time. So why has the company invested so much in Veza? And why is identity-related security such a priority in 2026? This Now on Now session shares how Veza is helping to tackle one of the fastest-emerging attack surfaces in the enterprise – non-human identities (NHIs).

Add to agenda

Gen Z vs Gen AI: Preparing the Next Generation for an AI-Powered Workforce

If there’s one generation that has experienced technology like no other, it is Gen Z. This generation is native to digital culture and grew up surrounded by technological innovations, all while preparing for a new AI-driven future. 

In this session, hear what students think about the pros and cons of AI, how education can prepare them, and how organizations and platforms can keep pathways into the digital economy open.

“This session focuses on how the next generation is preparing for an AI-driven future. It highlights how ServiceNow partnerships with academic institutions are helping bridge skill gaps through real-world exposure,” said Atul Grover, a ServiceNow MVP and Platform Owner.

Add to agenda

Keynote: Welcome to Agentic Business

ServiceNow is going all in on AI, recently announcing its move to a fully integrated, AI-native platform where AI, data, workflows, security, and governance are embedded by default across every product and package. 

In this keynote, ServiceNow Chairman and CEO, Bill McDermott, is joined by other ServiceNow execs and special guests to see how organizations are putting AI control front and center.

Add to agenda

“We are super excited about the AI infused everywhere strategy – and how customers can really turn that into a reality. The new ESM Foundation is super exciting as it opens up ServiceNow to a whole new cohort of organizations – possibly tens of thousands of net new organizations around the world,” said Chris Gabriel, executive and head of marketing and innovation at NTT Data. 

“And our big theme is around AI Control Tower and how the big bang of AI is expanding and why ServiceNow platform owners are in the perfect position to control it – in fact their business needs them to.”

Ask ServiceNow EmployeeWorks: Building the AI Front Door for Work

ServiceNow EmployeeWorks is an AI-powered “conversational front door” designed to unify employee interactions, enterprise search, and autonomous workflow execution across the ServiceNow AI platform. 

This session will discuss the role developers play in building and extending tools and enabling AI agents to act across applications.

Add to agenda

Day 2: Wednesday, May 6

From Prompt to Production: Enterprise Vibe Coding With the Build Agent

Vibe coding – the practice of using AI to build applications – was Collins Dictionary’s word of the year in 2025. This session explores how natural language prompts are replacing traditional development workflows, enabling builders of all skill levels to go from idea to working application faster than ever.

Add to agenda

A Conversation With WomenNow Leaders: The Unspoken Power of Women’s Soft Skills

Success in tech can’t be reduced to knowing how to code – it’s also about understanding people. In this discussion, the WomenNow leadership team explores how emotional intelligence, adaptability, and communication can help women at every level advance their careers, lead teams, and drive innovation.

“I’m really looking forward to attending this session because I strongly believe in supporting and uplifting women in tech. Recently, I hosted a women-only meetup where we had very open and honest conversations – ranging from dealing with anxiety and burnout to navigating careers while being new moms or managing family responsibilities,” said Shreya Wani, a 3x ServiceNow MVP.

“That experience reinforced for me that success in tech goes far beyond technical skills. It’s about empathy, communication, and truly understanding people. This session resonates deeply with that belief, and I’m excited to gain more insights on how we can leverage these strengths to grow, lead, and support each other more effectively in our careers.”

Add to agenda

Ask Me Anything: AI Control Tower

AI governance is already on the radar of risk and compliance teams. ServiceNow contends that its AI Control Tower gives organizations visibility and control over the AI models and agents running across the enterprise. Here’s an opportunity to sit down with its AI Control Tower experts and ask them anything in an open Q&A.

Add to agenda

“Knowledge is going to be chock-full of two major things in my humble opinion: platform governance and AI governance with AI Control Tower, and building your future with Build Agent on the platform.”

Jeremy Duncan – Principal Solution Architect, ServiceNow

Autonomous IT: A Zero-Touch Service Desk Built on Trust and Speed

Here, ServiceNow asks you to imagine a world where enterprise-wide investigations are automatically performed and diagnosed in seconds. Incidents are deflected before any issue is detected, or sorted within seconds after being created. Where actions are taken without any human intervention. Featuring Tanium, this session explores the world of Autonomous IT – a zero-touch Service Desk fueled by real-time visibility and control with intelligent, agentic workflows.

Add to agenda

Exchange Roundtable: CMDB/CSDM Foundation – Build It Right or Fix It Now

Your CMDB foundation is essential to get right for AI, automation, and product deployments. Making the decisions around it can be daunting, such as knowing when to start from scratch or clean up the mess you already have.

This session will help inform your way through these challenges, by discussing with the ServiceNow experts who have been through it all before.

Add to agenda

Day 3: Thursday, May 7

Discovery to AI Agents: Turning Process Insights Into Intelligent Automation

For the last day at Knowledge, get back to basics. In this Ask the Experts session, you can see how ServiceNow connects Process Mining, Task Mining, and Automation Center to automatically identify bottlenecks, analyze task steps, and generate AI Agents with one click. 

Learn how sequence-of-steps data informs Automation Requests, providing governance, time-motion insights, and supporting agent creation.

Add to agenda

Now on Now: Drive Action With CMDB, CSDM and Service Mapping

In this session, ServiceNow will explore its use of CMDB, CSDM, and service mapping, highlighting their critical role in IT operations. 

By better integrating these features, find out how to improve visibility, streamline processes, and enhance service delivery, driving greater operational efficiency.

Add to agenda

Final Thoughts

Knowledge 2026 looks set to be another defining moment for the ServiceNow ecosystem, with AI transformation, platform innovation, and real-world customer success front and center. 

Whether you’re attending in Las Vegas or tuning in remotely, the best approach is simple: plan ahead, stay flexible, and make time for the sessions that will deliver the most value when you get back to work.

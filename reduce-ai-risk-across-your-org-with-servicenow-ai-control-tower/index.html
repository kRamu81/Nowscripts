Reduce AI Risk Across Your Org With ServiceNow AI Control Tower

By Saima Ahmed

June 01, 2026

As enterprises accelerate the adoption of generative AI and AI agents, governance is becoming mission-critical. 

Join us for an in-depth look at ServiceNow’s AI Control Tower, the centralized hub designed to help teams monitor, manage, and control AI assets across the organization.

In this webinar, you’ll learn:

  • How ServiceNow AI Control Tower supports centralized AI governance, visibility, and control across the enterprise.
  • Best practices for managing the full lifecycle of AI assets, from discovery and ownership to compliance and risk mitigation.
  • How AI Asset Management works in practice, including a live demo of key capabilities and governance workflows.

Whether you lead AI strategy, platform architecture, IT governance, or compliance, this session will help you understand how to reduce AI risk while scaling innovation responsibly.

ServiceNow Certifications: Your Complete Guide

By Henry Martin

May 05, 2025

The ServiceNow ecosystem can be an exciting place to be, but, especially for newcomers, it can be somewhat overwhelming with the sheer amount of certifications available at the moment. 

With this in mind, we’ve put together a handy guide for mainline and expert certifications that help candidates prove their skills and technical knowledge of the ServiceNow Platform.

ServiceNow Mainline Certifications

Certified System Administrator (CSA)

This certification is for those who want to demonstrate the skills and essential knowledge to contribute to the configuration, implementation, and maintenance of the ServiceNow system. 

Those who pass the CSA Exam – which is a prerequisite for advanced courses – can boast of a set of skills necessary to continue on ServiceNow certification paths. 

If you want to train for the certification, you should take the Welcome to ServiceNow and ServiceNow Administration Fundamentals courses on the ServiceNow Learning Portal.

The following experience is also recommended: 

  • Experience with industry database concepts and system management.
  • A role as a system administrator and/or access to ServiceNow administrative applications and modules.
  • An understanding of IT Help Desk processes and incident, problem, and change workflows is helpful.
  • Three to six months of experience using and/or maintaining a ServiceNow instance.
  • A familiarity with industry terminology, acronyms, and initialisms.

The exam consists of 60 questions, in a multiple-choice or multiple-select answer format. It lasts 90 minutes.

The exam voucher cost is $300 USD if you take the on-demand learning path. Partner and customer discounts may apply.

ServiceNow certifications are based on a specific ServiceNow release. When a new release is launched, you have to keep your certification current, meaning you must participate in the Delta Exams and Maintenance Fee.

Certified Application Developer (CAD)

With the Certified Application Developer certification, you can demonstrate that you have the skills and essential knowledge to develop applications to solve business problems, both for employers and for sharing with the ServiceNow ecosystem. 

Your pre-requisite training should include the Welcome to ServiceNow on-demand course.

The required training for the CAD Exam is:

  • Scripting in ServiceNow Fundamentals
  • Application Development Fundamentals (after completion of this class, you are eligible for the CAD Exam voucher)

It is recommended that you have at least six months of hands-on experience developing applications in ServiceNow. 

Successful candidates will have application development and system administration roles and be a part of groups that let developers access ServiceNow. Candidates should also have general familiarity with industry terminology, acronyms, and initialisms.

The exam is 60 questions, with a multiple-choice or multiple-select answer format. The exam duration is 90 minutes, and the exam voucher costs $300 USD.

Certified Implementation Specialist – Platform Analytics (CIS-PA)

This certification will show that you have the skills to perform the configuration, implementation, and maintenance of a ServiceNow Platform Analytics solution.

Pre-requisite training includes: 

  • Welcome to ServiceNow
  • ServiceNow Administration Fundamentals 
  • Get Started with Now Create

The following is required training for the exam: 

  • Performance Analytics Essentials
  • Performance Analytics Fundamentals 
  • Get Started with Performance Analytics KPI Details
  • Formula Indicators Overview 
  • Performance Analytics (PA) Widgets
  • Responsive Dashboards Overview 
  • Performance Analytics Implementation Simulators 
  • Performance Analytics (PA) Advanced (after completion of this class, you are eligible for the CIS-PA Exam voucher)

Candidates should have six months of field experience participating in ServiceNow Performance Analytics implementation projects, deploying out-of-the-box content along with building custom reports and Performance Analytics objects like KPIs, widgets, dashboards, and collection jobs. 

The exam has 60 questions in a multiple-choice or multiple-select answer format. It lasts 90 minutes. The exam voucher costs $450 USD.

Certified Implementation Specialist – Discovery (CIS-DISCO) 

Successful candidates will be able to demonstrate they have the skills and essential knowledge to contribute to configuring, administering, implementing, and maintaining the Discovery application within the ServiceNow platform. 

Pre-requisite training for the certification includes: 

  • Welcome to ServiceNow 
  • ServiceNow Administration Fundamentals
  • Get Started with Now Create 
  • ServiceNow Platform Implementation
  • CMDB Fundamentals

The required training for the CIS-DISCO Exam includes: 

  • Discovery Fundamentals (after completion of this course, you are eligible for the CIS-DISCO Exam voucher)

It is recommended that candidates have intermediate or above skills in Windows and Unix administration, SNMP query knowledge, and JavaScript and regular expression scripting. Introductory or above network administration knowledge also helps. 

Six months of field experience participating in implementing ServiceNow ITOM solutions, specifically Discovery, is also recommended, as is participation in at least two ServiceNow Discovery deployments.

The exam has 45 questions in a multiple-choice or multiple-select answer format. It is 90 minutes long, and the exam voucher costs $450 USD.

ServiceNow Expert Certifications

Certified Technical Architect (CTA)

A ServiceNow Certified Technical Architect makes sure a business’s goals are achieved by translating technical requirements into architectural solutions.

The CTA has a hand in governance, designing platform models, and making sure their business’s needs are properly aligned with ServiceNow’s capabilities.

You need at least three years’ experience in implementing ServiceNow, along with a history of converting business requirements into ServiceNow platform capabilities. 

Applicants will need to have the following certifications:

  • Certified System Administrator (CSA)
  • Certified Application Developer (CAD)
  • Any two Certified Implementation Specialist (CIS) disciplines

The fee is $7,000 USD, payable after accepting the program offer.

Certified Master Architect (CMA)

The Certified Master Architect is, simply put, a ServiceNow expert. They are expected to be a trusted technical advisor and provide guidance on technology strategy, governance, architecture, and solution design. 

They guide customers along the ServiceNow journey, supporting them in aspects ranging from strategy development to execution. 

Those who want to take the CMA certification need at least five years of ServiceNow consulting experience, to have worked as a technical lead in more than six large-scale ServiceNow customer implementations, and to have experience in working with senior stakeholders, leading technical solutions, and presenting to large audiences.

To be eligible, you need a minimum of the following certifications:

  • Certified System Administrator (CSA)
  • ITSM Certified Implementation Specialist (CIS-ITSM)
  • Certified Application Developer (CAD)
  • Any two other Certified Implementation Specialist certifications (CIS in CSM, HR, SecOps, ITOM, etc.)
  • ITIL v3.0 or v4.0 Foundation (recommended)

The program fee is $17,000, payable following acceptance of the offer.

Summary

There are a lot of certifications out there for people looking to enter the ServiceNow ecosystem and demonstrate the skills they are building while working with the platform.

Hopefully, our guide has illuminated the differences between the myriad of qualifications on offer and narrowed them down for anyone interested in taking them.

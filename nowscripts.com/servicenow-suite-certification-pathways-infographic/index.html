ServiceNow Suite Certification Pathways Infographic 2026

By Ali Saghaeian

February 20, 2026

Going through ServiceNow certifications and the numerous available options can feel overwhelming. There is so much to consider, and it can be difficult to find a concise “big picture” overview. For this reason, I’ve put together this infographic to show how the different pieces come together and what a good starting point is. 

There are so many pathways across key applications, topics, and industries, but I simplified them by grouping and organizing them. You can see different color codes for certification types and their connecting arrows to make it easier for you to follow and find your way. You can also find NowScripts’s ServiceNow certification pathways infographic, which focuses on mainline and expert certifications.

ServiceNow offers a wide range of credentials, from micro-certifications and delivery accreditations to high-stakes (mainline) and suite certifications. You can also take bootcamp accreditations and go all the way to expert certifications.

While you need to study, practice, and take an exam for most of these credentials, suite certifications are automatically awarded if you follow a particular path. They’re mostly a combination of high-stakes with micro-certifications and/or delivery accreditations.

There is no order for completing the prerequisites for a Suite Certification. You will receive one once all required certifications are achieved. These certifications are grouped into three main categories: Core Skills Credential, Pro Credential Suites, and Pro Plus Credential Suites. I’ll cover these in detail.

If you already hold your CSA, CSM, ITSM, HR, HAM, or Data Foundations certification, you’re on the right path to take the next step to achieve a suite certification. Even if you have none of the high stakes, there is a path for you.

If you’re early in your ServiceNow journey, there is a quick win with the Core Skills Credential pathway, where you are only required to achieve a few micro-certifications. And, with specific Pro Credential Suites, you can complete a Now Assist accreditation and achieve the Pro Plus Credential Suites.

Use this guide as a reference, based on your interests and the career progression you expect. Not everything here is for you, and there may be very few who are experts in all these topics. So, feel free to skip those sections that are not relevant to you (yet) and focus on those that keep you excited!

Golden Bundles

There are some micro-certificates that I would like to call golden bundles.

For example, the following bundle of micro-certifications leads to seven Pro Suite and three Pro Plus Suite certifications: Platform Analytics, Predictive Intelligence, and Virtual Agent.

And with the following bundle, you’re one step closer to four Pro Suite certifications: Flows, Integration Hub, and UI Builder.

You achieve them all, and you’re on the path to 14 out of 20 Suite certificates.

Suite Certification Pathways

Here is a detailed guide on each of the suite certification pathways, contained within dropdown sections – click on the relevant sections depending on where you’re getting started.

Application Developer Core Skills

This suite certification shows you’re good at building modern applications on the platform. By achieving the golden bundle of Flows, Integration Hub, and UI Builder alongside the Automated Test Framework (ATF), you demonstrate that you can create sleek, automated, and thoroughly tested user journeys. If you are in development, this is a great starting point to bundle your accomplishments into a single suite. It’s a major achievement that shows you can build custom portal experiences and handle integrations without getting lost in extensive custom coding.

  • With the Automated Test Framework (ATF) Micro-Certification, you can create, execute, and manage automated tests to verify ServiceNow instance functionality. This is an area that I’ve been following for years, and I enjoyed exploring the new content packs. If you take the ATF Essentials course, you’ll learn how to run tests and test suites, create and schedule them, and the tips and techniques to administer them. The certification shows your proficiency with ATF, which results in accelerated testing and decreased manual effort.
  • With the Flows Micro-Certification, you are prepared to build, troubleshoot, and manage automated flows and business logic using the Workflow Studio interface. This is one of the most important areas for developers, as it allows you to build, refine, and redesign workflows across the entire platform. The certification demonstrates your ability to automate processes without complicated scripting by using triggers, actions, and flow logic.
  • With the Integration Hub Micro-Certification, you can start building and managing integrations by connecting ServiceNow to external systems using Integration Hub spokes, actions, and data stream actions. If you enjoy integrations, this is the area you would enjoy exploring. The certification demonstrates your ability to automate cross-platform processes and manage advanced data transfers without extensive custom coding.
  • With the UI Builder Micro-Certification, you are ready to create and configure web-based workspaces and custom portal experiences using the UI Builder and Next Experience components. There is also a fundamentals course that guides you through creating and configuring a workspace using UI Builder. You’ll be able to create pages and page variants within an experience, and configure components within a page. The certification shows your proficiency in designing modern user interfaces, managing data resources, and handling events to deliver bespoke user journeys.

Workflow Data Fabric Professional

This suite certification is your way to excelling in enterprise-level automation and data orchestration. It shows you have the skills to design and monitor automation requests across the board, from deploying Robotic Process Automation (RPA) to managing high-volume Kafka data streams. By achieving this, you present your ability to analyze business processes for bottlenecks and optimize workflow performance, effectively turning data into an actionable asset. It requires the Integration Hub Micro-Certification along with a few delivery accreditations.

  • With the Automation Center Delivery Accreditation, you are prepared to design, plan, and monitor automation requests while integrating third-party tools within the Automation Center. It includes an overview course that helps organizations accelerate automation at scale. You can also see how the components of Automation Center fit into the larger CMDB framework. The accreditation shows that you can manage automation performance and extract business insights from dashboards to track time and cost savings.
  • With the Process Mining Essentials Delivery Accreditation, you can analyze and visualize business processes using audit data to detect bottlenecks and redundancies within the platform. This includes a course that covers key features and personas, shows how to mine your projects, and explains how to analyze event data to improve your processes. It was a great intro lesson for me when I took it. You can also get a hands-on experience using the Process Configuration Builder to set it up for different tables and configure your mining project accordingly. If you are keen or curious like me to explore further, there is also an advanced Process Mining path and certification that dives deeper and becomes more practical. Having the accreditation demonstrates your ability to configure mining projects, interpret process maps, and use AI-powered insights to drive continual improvement and optimize workflow performance.
  • With the Robotic Process Automation (RPA) Delivery Accreditation, you can start designing, configuring, and deploying both attended and unattended robots using the Design Studio and RPA Hub. There is also an essentials course that covers the initial setup, building, deploying, and managing robots. This accreditation shows your knowledge of automating common, high-volume manual tasks and integrating them into wider ServiceNow workflows to increase productivity and reduce errors.
  • With the Stream Connect for Apache Kafka Delivery Accreditation, you are prepared to configure and manage integrations between ServiceNow and Apache Kafka to process high-volume, live data streams. The accreditation can demonstrate your competency in producing and consuming Kafka messages, managing message namespaces, and utilizing Hermes for resilient data streaming.

Read more about Workflow Data Fabric Professional.

Accounts Payable Operations

This suite certification validates your knowledge in supporting and transforming the financial lifecycle in ServiceNow. It proves you can implement the Accounts Payable Operations application to automate invoice ingestion and handle secure stakeholder communications within the Source-to-Pay Workspace. With this credential, you demonstrate that you can create a unified employee experience while effectively managing the end-to-end invoice lifecycle. You will need to achieve the golden bundle of Flows, Integration Hub, and UI Builder, along with a couple of micro-certifications and delivery accreditations.

  • With the Welcome to ServiceNow Micro-Certification, you can comfortably navigate the Next Experience UI, manage tasks via lists and forms, and gain the fundamental skills to perform common tasks, manage records, and use some core platform capabilities such as Platform Analytics, Knowledge Management, and Service Catalog. A while back, I decided to refresh my knowledge by taking this introductory course and completing its assessment simulator after a decade working with ServiceNow, and I really enjoyed it. I recommend this beginner course and certification to anyone new or eager to start their ServiceNow journey.
  • With the Service Portal Micro-Certification, you are prepared to configure, customize, and extend components to create an intuitive user experience within the Service Portal. This includes a foundational course on portal development and administration, with numerous labs to complete for hands-on experience. It also takes you to a path to create a new Service Portal with all the required widgets, menus, and layout. You get to use some of the advanced capabilities and even configure AI Search for an enhanced result. The certification demonstrates your ability to build themes, pages, and widgets while managing search capabilities and portal functionality to meet business needs.
  • With the Employee Center Delivery Accreditation, you can implement and configure the Employee Center, including managing taxonomies, widgets, and portal branding. It comes with an essential course for everyone working on the front-facing side of the platform. It takes you through the implementation steps, covering baseline configurations, and shows you how to manage it efficiently. There are also labs that let you practice different capabilities. I personally enjoy working on Employee Center as a single place that provides employees with access to everything they need for their day-to-day work. Having the accreditation indicates your ability to migrate from Service Portal and establish governance and roles to deliver a unified employee experience.
  • With the Accounts Payable Operations (APO) Delivery Accreditation, you are positioned to implement and configure the APO application, with the focus on the technical and operational requirements for managing the end-to-end invoice lifecycle. It highlights your ability to automate invoice ingestion and data extraction using Document Intelligence, and to configure the Source-to-Pay Workspace to resolve exceptions and manage stakeholder communication.

This suite certification shows your proficiency in improving complex procurement workflows from start to finish. By learning the ShoppingHub and the sourcing request lifecycle, you can automate the source-to-pay process and drive great efficiency gains. This certification highlights your ability to use the Source-to-Pay Workspace to manage supplier communication and deliver a modern, intuitive user experience for organizational procurement. You will need to achieve the golden bundle of Flows, Integration Hub, and UI Builder, along with the Welcome to ServiceNow and Service Portal micro-certifications, plus a couple of delivery accreditations, including the Employee Center.

  • The main one is the Sourcing and Procurement Operations (SPO) Delivery Accreditation, which verifies your ability to implement and configure the SPO application, including setting up the ShoppingHub and managing sourcing request workflows. It demonstrates your ability to automate the source-to-pay lifecycle, streamline supplier communication, and use the Source-to-Pay Workspace to drive procurement efficiency.

By holding this certification, you can showcase your ability to utilize the Supplier Collaboration Portal and ShoppingHub to improve communication and maintain data integrity across the platform. You will need to achieve the golden bundle of Flows, Integration Hub, and UI Builder, along with the Welcome to ServiceNow and Service Portal micro-certifications, plus a particular delivery accreditation.

Data Foundations (CMDB and CSDM) Professional

This suite certification is important for anyone serious about platform integrity, as it helps you design and maintain a high-quality CMDB aligned with the Common Service Data Model (CSDM). It demonstrates your skills in using the CI Class Manager to set up identification and reconciliation rules while monitoring health metrics, such as compliance. Achieving this confirms you can identify and remediate data issues, ensuring the foundation of your ServiceNow instance is reliable and accurate.

  • With the CMDB Health Micro-Certification, you are ready to configure and maintain the CMDB Health Dashboard by setting up metrics for completeness, compliance, and correctness. I took it while completing the CMDB fundamentals course. To achieve the certification, you will need to complete an assessment simulator as the exam. It demonstrates your ability to identify and remediate data integrity issues, assuring a reliable, high-quality Configuration Management Database.
  • With the Configure the CMDB Micro-Certification, you are prepared to design and structure the CMDB by using the CI Class Manager and creating identification and reconciliation rules. For me, it felt easier to take it together with CMDB Health, since they were both embedded in the CMDB fundamentals course. And similar to the CMDB Health credential, you will need to complete some assessment simulator tasks to achieve the certification. It demonstrates your competence in managing data sources and sustaining data integrity through Multisource CMDB and data refresh rules.

ITSM Professional

This suite certification proves you can take IT Service Management to the next level by utilizing AI and analytics via the golden bundle of Platform Analytics, Predictive Intelligence, and Virtual Agent. This was the first suite certification I aimed for when it was introduced, as it brought together some key platform capabilities with the ITSM products. I can clearly see how much they helped me along the way. Having the suite shows you are good at these capabilities, including your proficiency in building automated chat conversations and using machine learning to categorize incidents and predict field values automatically. By achieving this credential, you can design real-time dashboards that monitor and forecast business performance, significantly improving organizational efficiency.

  • With the Platform Analytics Micro-Certification, you are equipped to configure and manage Platform Analytics by setting up indicator sources, automated indicators, and breakdowns. This was one of the first certifications I completed, and I enjoyed every bit of it. Historically, it covered the basics of Performance Analytics. It then evolved to include Data Visualizations and Dashboards. This is also a good starting point if you are aiming for the Certified Implementation Specialist in Platform Analytics (CIS-PA). The micro-certification showcases your ability to design and share in-line dashboards and data visualizations to monitor, forecast, and optimize business process performance.
  • With the Predictive Intelligence Micro-Certification, you are prepared to configure and administer machine learning solutions within the ServiceNow platform using frameworks like classification, similarity, and clustering. This is one of those AI areas that existed on the platform for many years and evolved fast. I had the opportunity to put some of the capabilities into practice and realize the actual benefit. The certification shows your ability to automate data-based decisions, such as auto-categorizing incidents and predicting field values, to improve organizational efficiency.
  • With the Virtual Agent Micro-Certification, you are ready to implement and maintain the ServiceNow Virtual Agent and Natural Language Understanding (NLU) models. I’ve always enjoyed working with chatbots and how they evolved with NLU and, later, AI. I have learned a great deal since I began working with Virtual Agent and its capabilities. This micro-certification uses an assessment simulator as the exam to validate your ability to build automated chat conversations, manage branding and settings, and create intents and entities to improve the user self-service experience.

Final Thoughts  

Whether you’ve recently started your ServiceNow journey or you’re an experienced pro, the suite certifications offer a massive opportunity to validate your expertise across the platform. If you already hold high-stakes certifications like your CSA, CSM, or ITSM, you are already on the right path to achieving multiple suites automatically just by adding a handful of strategic micro-certifications. 

My recommendation is to focus on the “golden bundles”, specifically Platform Analytics, Predictive Intelligence, and Virtual Agent, or the trio of Flows, Integration Hub, and UI Builder. Learning them allows you to unlock the bigger portion of the 20 available suite certificates, giving you a competitive edge.

For those looking to reach the top tier, aim for the Pro Plus Credential Suites by completing the relevant Now Assist accreditations to present your skills in Generative AI. It is a quick win that proves you can keep up with the latest platform innovations as you build a good professional profile. 

No matter which path you choose, the important thing is to keep your prerequisite certifications up to date with the annual delta exams so your hard-earned suite certifications remain current and valid.

As a final note, while certifications are a great way to showcase your domain knowledge and achievements, they don’t necessarily guarantee success in the real world. To truly be considered an expert and stay competitive in today’s market, you need a significant amount of practice and hands-on experience. I highly recommend aiming for a certification once you already have a solid foundation in that domain, because simply gaining the knowledge to pass an exam doesn’t guarantee you can apply those skills to solve actual business problems or deal with complex, real-life scenarios.

Wishing you all the best in your ServiceNow certification journey!
